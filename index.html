Privacy Policy for scrubDEX

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

scrubDEX is a surgical logging and journaling application intended for personal record-keeping, self-audit, and organization. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled within the app.

1. Data Collection

scrubDEX does not collect personal or sensitive user data on its own servers.

  • No personal information (such as name, email address, or phone number) is collected by the developer
  • No usage analytics are collected
  • No crash reports are collected
  • No device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data are collected
  • No data is tracked, profiled, or monetized

2. Data Storage and Processing

By default, all data entered into scrubDEX is stored locally on the user’s device only.

Local Storage

  • Data remains on the user’s device
  • The developer has no access to locally stored data

Google Drive Backup (Optional)

scrubDEX provides an optional, user-initiated Google Drive backup feature.

  • Google Drive backup is entirely optional and initiated by the user
  • Data is backed up to the user’s own Google Drive account
  • Data is transferred directly between the app and Google Drive
  • No scrubDEX servers are used for storage or processing
  • The developer does not access, view, or receive Google Drive backup data

Use of Google Drive is governed by Google’s own privacy policies.

3. Data Export

scrubDEX allows users to export their data in JSON or CSV format.

  • Exports are generated locally on the device
  • Exported files are saved or shared at the user’s discretion
  • The developer does not receive or transmit exported data

4. Data Sharing

scrubDEX does not sell, rent, share, or disclose user data to third parties.

  • No advertising networks are used
  • No analytics services are used
  • No crash reporting services are used

If the user enables Google Drive backup, data is shared only with Google Drive under the user’s control and Google’s privacy terms. The developer does not access this data.

5. Permissions

scrubDEX may request certain permissions (such as storage access or Google Drive access) strictly to support app functionality.

  • Permissions are requested only when required
  • All permission usage is initiated by the user
  • Data accessed through permissions is not transmitted to the developer

6. Medical Disclaimer

scrubDEX is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations.

  • The app is intended solely for logging and personal record-keeping
  • It does not guide or influence clinical decision-making
  • It should not be used as a substitute for professional medical judgment, institutional records, or official documentation

Users are solely responsible for how information recorded in the app is used.

7. User Control and Data Deletion

Users retain full control over their data at all times.

  • Entries can be added, edited, or deleted within the app
  • Backups can be enabled or disabled by the user
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data
  • Google Drive backups can be deleted by the user from their Google Drive account

The developer cannot recover deleted data.

8. Children’s Privacy

scrubDEX is not intended for use by children under the age of 18.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Third-Party Services

scrubDEX integrates only the following third-party service:

  • Google Drive — used solely for optional, user-initiated data backup

scrubDEX does not integrate advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or tracking services.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as features evolve or legal requirements change. Any updates will be reflected by revising the effective date above.
