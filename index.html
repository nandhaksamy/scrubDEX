Privacy Policy for scrubDEX

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

scrubDEX is a surgical logging and journaling application designed for personal record-keeping and self-audit. This Privacy Policy explains how user data is handled.

1. Data Collection

scrubDEX does not collect any personal or sensitive user data.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics are collected
  • No crash reports are collected
  • No identifiers such as name, email, device ID, IP address, or location are collected

2. Data Storage and Processing

All data entered into scrubDEX is stored locally on the user’s device only.

  • Data never leaves the device
  • No cloud storage or synchronization is used
  • No servers are used for data storage or processing
  • The developer has no access to user data

3. Data Sharing

scrubDEX does not share, sell, or disclose any data to third parties.

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no data to share.

4. Permissions

Any permissions requested by the app (such as file access, if applicable) are used only to support app functionality and operate entirely on-device.

No data accessed through permissions is transmitted outside the device.

5. Medical Disclaimer

scrubDEX is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations.

  • The app is intended solely for logging and personal record-keeping
  • It does not guide or influence clinical decision-making
  • It should not be used as a substitute for professional medical judgment, institutional records, or official documentation

Users are solely responsible for how they use information recorded in the app.

6. User Control

Users have full control over their data.

  • Entries can be added, edited, or deleted at any time
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data

7. Children’s Privacy

scrubDEX is not intended for use by children under the age of 18.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
