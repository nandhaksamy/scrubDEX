ScrubDex

Cases. Tasks. Insights.

ScrubDex is a clinician-built surgical logbook and task management system designed for procedural professionals who need structure without added complexity.

Training is demanding. Clinical workload is relentless. Cases accumulate, follow-ups multiply, and meaningful progress becomes harder to track. ScrubDex helps restore clarity and control.

Core Capabilities

  • Structured Case Logging – Document procedures, diagnoses, roles, and notes in organized, searchable entries.
  • Task & Reminder Tracking – Manage follow-ups, ward duties, and case-related actions with precision.
  • Practice Insights – Review trends in case volume and procedural exposure over time.
  • Offline-First Architecture – Fully functional without internet access. Your data remains on your device.
  • Export & Backup – Generate structured reports and create secure backups of your records.

Data & Google Drive Access

ScrubDex stores all case data locally on your device. The developer does not operate backend servers for case storage.

An optional Google Drive backup feature is available. When enabled, the app accesses only the files it creates within your Google Drive account to allow you to store encrypted backups. It does not access or modify other files in your Drive.

Backup is entirely user-initiated and can be disabled at any time.
